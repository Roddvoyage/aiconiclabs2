FAQ

How does it work?

+

Our platform generates hyper-realistic AI models tailored to your brand’s vision. Instead of relying on traditional models for photoshoots or campaigns, we create AI-generated models that can be customized to fit any style, look, or demographic. Whether you need a diverse range of models or a consistent brand ambassador, we provide the AI models you need without the constraints of logistics or availability.

Why use AI-generated models?

+

AI models offer unmatched flexibility and cost-efficiency. You no longer need to schedule shoots, hire models, or manage production logistics. Our AI-generated models can be adapted to any setting, outfit, or pose, saving you time and resources while providing endless creative possibilities. Plus, AI models can be used repeatedly and modified as needed, ensuring your brand has full control.

Who can benefit from AI-generated models?

+

Any brand that relies on visual content—whether in fashion, e-commerce, advertising, or entertainment—can benefit. If you need models for product displays, social media campaigns, lookbooks, or advertisements, AI models provide a fast, scalable, and cost-effective alternative.

How customizable are the AI models?

+

Our AI models are fully customizable. You can select their appearance, expressions, poses, and even specific body types to match your brand’s aesthetic. The models can be adjusted in real-time to fit different settings or campaigns, and we offer a wide variety of looks to ensure diversity and representation.

What is the pricing?

+

We offer flexible pricing based on your needs:

Basic: Ideal for small campaigns or test runs, starting at $100 per image/model.

Advanced: For larger campaigns with multiple AI models or ongoing use, starting at $8000/year.

Enterprise: For brands needing high-scale, fully customized AI models, pricing on request. We also offer special packages for recurring content needs or large volume projects.

Who is on the team?

+

Our team includes AI specialists, creative directors, and visual artists who work together to develop the most realistic AI models possible. We combine cutting-edge technology with a deep understanding of fashion, branding, and visual design to deliver AI models that resonate with your audience

Do I need technical expertise to use AI models?

+

No technical expertise is required. We make it easy for you to integrate AI models into your campaigns. Simply provide your creative brief, and we handle the rest. Our platform is user-friendly, and our team is available to assist with any technical or creative needs.

How do you ensure quality and realism?

+

Our AI-generated models are designed to be highly realistic, thanks to advanced neural networks and machine learning algorithms. We continuously refine the models based on real-world data to ensure they meet the highest standards of photorealism, capturing intricate details such as skin texture, lighting, and clothing movement.

Can I try the AI models before committing?

+

Absolutely! We offer trial packages where you can test our AI-generated models for your brand’s content. This allows you to experience the quality and flexibility of our models before fully committing to a larger project.

What kind of support is available post-deployment?

+

We offer continuous support and updates to ensure your AI models stay aligned with your brand’s needs. Whether you require new model features, additional customization, or technical assistance, our team is here to help.
